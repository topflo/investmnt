Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2024

1. Introduction

At WealthBridge, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name and contact details
  • Date of birth and identification documents
  • Financial information
  • Investment preferences

Technical Information

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and version
  • Usage patterns and preferences
  • Cookies and similar technologies

3. How We Use Your Information

Account Management

To create and manage your account, verify your identity, and provide customer support.

Investment Services

To process your investments, provide investment recommendations, and manage your portfolio.

Security

To protect your account, detect and prevent fraud, and ensure platform security.

Communication

To send important updates, market insights, and personalized investment opportunities.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Regular security audits
  • Secure data storage
  • Access controls and authentication

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Export your data
